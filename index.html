YouTube Partner Program Requirements (2026)

To qualify for YouTube monetization, your channel needs:

  • 1,000 Subscribers - Real, organic subscribers (no purchased or sub-for-sub)
  • 4,000 Watch Hours - Valid public watch hours in the last 12 months. Shorts do NOT count toward this.
  • No Active Strikes - Your channel must have no active Community Guidelines strikes
  • Linked AdSense Account - An active Google AdSense account linked to your channel

Meeting these numbers is only the first step. YouTube's reviewers also evaluate content originality, ad-friendliness, niche consistency, and many other signals beyond basic metrics. MonetizeCheck evaluates your channel against all these dimensions simultaneously and gives you a score out of 100 with a specific action plan.
