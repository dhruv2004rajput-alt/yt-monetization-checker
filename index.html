YouTube Partner Program Requirements (2026)

To qualify for YouTube monetization, your channel needs: 1,000 real organic subscribers, 4,000 valid public watch hours in the last 12 months, no active Community Guidelines strikes, and an active Google AdSense account linked to your channel.
