Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

MonetizeCheck does not collect, store, or save any personal information. YouTube channel URLs you submit are sent to our AI provider (Groq) for real-time analysis only and are immediately discarded after processing. No database, log, or record of your submission is maintained.

2. How We Use Information

Channel URLs submitted are used solely to generate your monetization eligibility analysis. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any data with third parties for marketing purposes. All analysis happens in real-time and no data persists beyond your browser session.

3. Data Retention

All analysis data is processed in real-time and immediately discarded. We maintain zero logs, zero databases, and zero user profiles. When you close your browser tab, your report is gone permanently.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but please note that disabling cookies may affect your experience with ads. MonetizeCheck does not use any other tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or session storage.
